Part 3 - Sign in

Time to get practical...

Step 1

The Yammer App is available free in the App Store on iPhone and iPad, or Google Play for Android Devices, download and install it. This App does get regularly updated, so do make sure you keep updating it when prompted. You’ll need to Sign-In once, after that your device will remember your details. If you have been using Yammer previously using a Shared Account, you will need to make sure you log out before you start, Slide open the menu and press Log out



Step 2

When you open the app you should come to the screen below, choose the Log In button



Step 3

• If you are a head office employee, international, regional management or store managers with a ‘named’ email address Enter your Work email as first.last@mnscorp.net, you can leave this password blank. E.g. joe.bloggs@mnscorp.net
• If you are a UK store employee Enter your username as employeeID@mnscorp.net, you can leave this password blank. E.g. 15040824@mnscorp.net.
• Press Log In.



Step 4

• If you are a head office employee, international, regional management or store managers with a ‘named’ email address: Enter your username as first.last@mnscorp.net and the normal password you use to log on to M&S computers. E.g. joe.bloggs@mnscorp.net
• If you are a UK store employee” Enter your username as employeeID@mnscorp.net and the normal password you would use to log onto a store workstation or HHT. E.g. 15040824@mnscorp.net



Step 5

That’s it, you’re in and can now view and post messages and pictures to Yammer!



Part 4 - Your profile

A completed Yammer profile lets your colleagues know who you are. As you are logging on using a generic email address your profile will be set up for your store rather than you as individual. Therefore whilst you can’t use your personal details you can make sure the Store Profile contains some key information on the store in general that will help others to understand the context of your comments. For example the type of Store you are, GM: Food ratio, customer profile and the region you sit within. Let people know the people that are accessing the account, for example Commercial Managers or Section Managers.
